Smartfridge

Our Smartfridge will be making meals with the ingredients in your house!

Description

Our Smartfridge can make food ideas based on your ingredients at home. It can also give you ideas for drinks such as milkshakes, homemade iced tea, etc.. The website is totally free!

Prerequisites: You just need internet!

And ofcourse food at home!
